NRL 2017–2021

Basic exploratory visualisations of National Rugby League regular-season and finals matches. Data: archive/nrldata.csv (973 matches).

1. Total wins per team

Counts every match where the team's score exceeded their opponent's. Draws excluded.

2. Wins per team per season

Heatmap shows how each team's win count shifted across the five seasons.

3. Home vs away wins

Stacked bars splitting wins by venue type.

4. Points scored vs points conceded

A team in the lower-right scores a lot but also lets a lot in; upper-left is the defensive end.

5. Match-score distribution

Histogram of every team-score across all matches (home and away pooled).

6. Average winning margin per season

Margin = |home score − away score|. Tracks how lopsided games have been over time.

7. Matches by region (map view)

Where matches were played. Most NRL games sit in NSW and QLD; 2020–2021 saw a Queensland surge due to COVID relocations.

8. Top 15 venues by matches hosted

9. Head-to-head matchup heatmap

Rows = home team, columns = away team, colour = number of matches played.

10. Match scoring by round

Average combined points (home + away) per round number, by season.

Built with Vega-Lite. Data covers NRL seasons 2017–2021. Charts are intentionally basic for early exploration — the final assignment will need 10 polished, annotated charts plus a proper choropleth map and a second data source.
